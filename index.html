Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our FAQs. If you have any other questions
you'd like answered please feel free to email us.

What is Bookmark?

A bookmark is a saved link to a webpage that you can quickly access later. It helps you keep track of important or frequently visited sites without needing to remember the URLs.

How can I request a new browser?

To request a new browser, visit the official website or app store and download it. Follow the installation instructions to set it up.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes, most popular browsers have mobile apps available on both Android and iOS. You can find them in your device's app store.

What about other other chromium browsers?

Other Chromium-based browsers like Edge and Brave offer similar features, with some added privacy and performance enhancements. They can be downloaded and used as alternatives to Chrome.
